## How It Works

### Click Behavior
1. **First click:** Chip turns **purple/red** (active), word **added** to prompt
2. **Second click:** Chip turns **white** (inactive), word **removed** from prompt
3. Words are **comma-separated** automatically
4. **Active chips** stay highlighted so you know what's in your prompt

### Hide/Show Section
- Click **"Hide"** button to collapse trigger words
- Click **"Show"** to expand again
- Useful for decluttering the interface
- Your active selections persist when hidden
